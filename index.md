This application does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users.

All user data (such as tasks or images) is stored locally on the user's device and is never transmitted to external servers.

This application uses third-party services such as Google AdMob to display advertisements. These services may collect anonymous data, including device identifiers, to deliver relevant ads.

We do not control how third-party services collect or use data. Please refer to their privacy policies for more information.

No personal information such as name, email, or contact information is collected by this application.
