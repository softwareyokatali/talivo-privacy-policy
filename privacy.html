Privacy Policy

Your data, your control.

Last updated · April 2026

Talivo does not use advertising SDKs, does not sell personal information, and does not collect personal data for marketing or tracking purposes.

Your tasks, images, and related app content are primarily stored on your device. If iCloud sync is enabled on your Apple account and supported by your device settings, your data may also be synchronized securely through Apple's iCloud services.

Talivo does not operate its own backend for storing your app content. When iCloud is used, synchronization and storage are handled by Apple under your Apple account and Apple's applicable terms and privacy practices.

This application does not include advertisements, subscription purchases, or premium membership systems in its current version. However, advertisements, premium features, or subscription options may be introduced in future updates. If such features are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Talivo does not require you to create an account and does not directly collect personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details through the app.
